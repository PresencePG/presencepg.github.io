From startups to large enterprises, financial services to health care, we collaborate with designers, developers, and our clients to build what really matters for their business.   • Ninefold

Macquarie Telecom set out to disrupt the IaaS and PaaS markets while expanding their market overseas.

Presence worked with Ninefold, a tiger team of Macquarie Telecom executives and developers, to identify a new target market, define the minimum viable product, and test their product concepts within the US market.

Ninefold has released their new platform as a service (PaaS) for Ruby to ecstatic developers in both Australia and the US.

Advent Software (ADVS) saw disruption of their enterprise software business from light weight cloud and mobile based products.

Presence worked with their team, across product lines, to reimagine their products for web and mobile devices. This involved refining product requirements, prioritizing product features, and implementing cloud and mobile versions of their new products alongside their internal teams.

At the first public product preview their stock jumped nearly 10%.

Brown & Toland Physicians wanted to revitalize and extend the functionality of their consumer web and mobile experience to better serve as a health resource.

Presence developed web and mobile products that deliver interactive health experiences for their consumer customers. Presence refined and prioritized product requirements and implemented web and mobile products.
